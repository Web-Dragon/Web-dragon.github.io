Twenty One Pilots is an American musical duo originating from Columbus, Ohio. The band was formed in 2009, and originally had more members. The original members consisted of Tyler Joseph, Nick Thomas and Chris Salih. However both Nick Thomas and Chris Salih left in 2011. Since then, it has been just Tyler Joseph and Josh Dun, the drummer of the duo.
